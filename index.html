Poe trademessagespammer

This is a quick and dirty trade message spammer for POE (Path of Exile). No error checking, and using inputmessage wrapper (http://inputsimulator.codeplex.com/). If you want to enter letters in a numeric field, go ahead.

This is a quick and dirty (no error trapping) trade message spammer that depends on http://inputsimulator.codeplex.com/ wrapper. Since that is released under MS-PL, inherently this is also of such a release. For my code, it is distributed under the I spent 5 min on it (aka I dont give a shit license or IDGAS).

Installing

1) Extract the zip to a folder of your choice DOWNLOAD IT FROM HERE
2) Locate the POETradeChatv1.zip and extract it to the folder you want to use it from.
2) Make sure the dll and the exe is in the same dir
3) Open the exe (Hey if you dont trust me compile it yourself, I am too lazy to post a virus scan)

Prerequisites

1) Make sure in game you DO NOT have anything in your chat (like a players name from your last message)
2) Make sure that your chat window does not have focus by closing the chat window

Usage

Well its very straighforward. Note that any time field is in milliseconds. I was too lazy to div by 1000.
